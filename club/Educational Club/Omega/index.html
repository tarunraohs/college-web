About Us

Welcome to Club Omega, A place to have fun with codes and let out the geek within you. We AIM to bring out the best coder within each individual. Conducting competitive Challenges, State and National level Hackathons in order to ignite team spirit and incorporate fun hands-on learning experience with programming, make coding a habit not a repetitive task and want students to have fun coding and present a platform for them to showcase their knowledge and talent in front of domain experts and industry leaders, help members receive endorsements from various Clubs, Companies, Organizations and Institutions.
