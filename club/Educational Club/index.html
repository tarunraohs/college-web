Educational Facilities

Education Club utilizes service projects, speakers, and activities to bring members of Benedictine College, education majors and other majors alike, together to become better builders of community. The club works with numerous groups throughout campus and the local community to impact aspects of education in a positive way. Education club strives to spread awareness about current issues in the education system and assists in making education a welcoming and successful community.
