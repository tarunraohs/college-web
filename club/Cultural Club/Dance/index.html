About Us

About Us

Dance club is to boost up the morale of students and to highlight the talent of dance and creativity. It provides opportunities to dance lovers to prove their talent and it is a good platform for them to have their artistic exploration. This club plays an important role in maintaining the positive environment in the university. By this Club, students get opportunities to represent PRESIDENCY UNIVERSITY in various reputed organizations.
