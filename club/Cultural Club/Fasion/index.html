About Us

About Us

The mission of Fashion Club is to encourage and facilitate activities that relate to the fashion industry. Students will learn about fashion career opportunities, participate in community service events, and assist with the spring fashion show. The objective of this club is to promote the students to know about the different areas in fashion, the changes in fashion, and the ability to enhance creative leadership skills and to encourage self-expression and uniqueness in fabric arts and craft skills.
