About Us

About Us

The Environment Cell is mainly to teach students about the importance of Natural Environment and motivate them to practice conservation of this planet which is a home for all of us, and to become conservation leaders. The club aims at inculcating values for preserving the fragile planet and ecosystem. There was a dire need of an association in the name of 'Environment cell in presidency university . This cells focus more on foster knowledge ,skill and values to protection of our environment and sustainable living , field visits to study flora of region and understand the importance of education .
