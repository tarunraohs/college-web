About Us

About Us

Rotaract is a service, leadership and community service organization for young men and women between the ages 18-30.Rotaract focuses on the development of young adults as leader in their communities and workplace through service. We at presidency started the charted club of Rotaract club at 2019 with 15 students and now the club has 140 students working where we focus on the Rural women , community , underprivileged children , slums , education to government school etc . We are proud to say that Rotaract club is more active in all the works they perform and the objective and a good cause they do to the society.
