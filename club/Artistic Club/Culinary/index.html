About Us

Cooking competitions are a means to taste the future of cooking. The aim of the competition is to discover new culinary talents from different schools of Presidency University. A cooking competition is an innovative way to increase student participation in work-based learning. Food always connects us and the students experienced it during this event and were overwhelmed with happiness.
