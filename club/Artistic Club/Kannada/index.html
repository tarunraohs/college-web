About Us

This club is about to teach students the importance of the Kannada language, its state , their culture and their way of living . The Kannada club of presidency university every year celebrates the kannada rajyotsava in a very grand way by welcoming the guests,celebrities and some cultural performances.To promote Kannada and its culture among students and staff of Presidency University.

KALA PRADARSHANA

On the Occasion of Karnataka Rajyotsava, Art and Sketch Club in association with the Kannada club of Presidency University organized an Art Exhibition- KALA PRADARSHANA in DSA lawn on 16th of November, 2022. The main aim is to provide limitless opportunities for the creative minds which were surely splendid.

RAJYOTSAVA FLASH MOB

Dance club of Presidency University in collaboration with Kannada club organised a Flash mob at the canopy area on 10th of November 2022.This flash mob was conducted as a promotional event for Karnataka Rajyotsava celebration which will be happening on 23rd November 2022. It was open to all the students of Presidency University.
