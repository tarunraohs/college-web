About Us

Everyone shares his/her own views through art. The display of art form is wonderful and much praised by many people. The main objective of the club is to cherish and value the different art forms. Students from different schools/departments have taken initiative with great passion to showcase their talent and display their artworks. Organizing a club like this has proved very beneficial for the art world and through these opportunities.
