TALENT HUNT

The Talent Hunt event was organized by Stand up Comedy in collaboration with the Speakers & Anchor club and the Theatre club of Presidency University on 28th of September 2022. The event was conducted in the Amphitheater, Near Cafeteria.

POINT OF VIEW

The Speakers and Anchors Club of Presidency University organized a group discussion, Point of View . The main aim of the event was to hear various perspectives on trending table topics and letting students put their point of view without judgment

POTPOURRI COMPETITION

The Department of Student Affairs, Presidency University organized a Literary Fest. The event Potpourri was hosted by Speakers and Anchors club.The event consisted of 3 rounds; Round 1 being Dumb Charades of the theme novels, plays and movies, round 2 consisting of tongue twisters and the final Round being a Quiz round.
