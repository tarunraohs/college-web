About Us

This club introduces a new idea, inspired participants to further explore it on their own, or may illustrate and promote actual process practice. It is a great way to teach hands-on skills as it gives learners an opportunity to try out new methods and fail in a safe environment. This kind of activity always helps students to discuss difficulties faced while editing and helps them to get involved and try to find the solution for the same.
